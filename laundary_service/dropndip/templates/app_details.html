DropNDrip - Smart Laundry Solution

Problem Statement

Busy professionals and immigrants in A-grade cities struggle with regular laundry due to time constraints, lack of access to machines, and expensive service options.

Solution

HydroJungle offers an affordable, hygienic door-to-door laundry service with multiple offerings: wash & fold, wash & iron, dry cleaning, and ironing only—aimed at convenience and quality.

Services Offered

  • Wash & Fold: ₹60–₹80/kg
  • Wash & Iron: ₹90–₹120/kg
  • Dry Cleaning: ₹100–₹300/item
  • Ironing Only: ₹10–₹20/piece
  • Special Items: ₹100–₹200/item (bedsheets, curtains, etc.)

Workflow

  1. Customer books via app or WhatsApp
  2. Pickup partner collects clothes
  3. Laundry processed at central facility
  4. Delivery within 24–48 hours

Business Model

  • Subscription: ₹299 – ₹499/month (4 pickups)
  • Pay-as-you-go options available

Technology

  • Mobile App and WhatsApp booking
  • Digital weight tracking
  • Online payments (UPI, GPay, etc.)

Challenges

  • Ensuring timely delivery
  • Maintaining quality control
  • Managing item loss/damage

Add-on Services

  • Shoe/Bag Cleaning
  • Perfumed Clothing Delivery
  • Monthly Wardrobe Refresh
